Social Media

Facebook

Facebook is a social network and we have a FB Page and FB Group. The Page is public while the FB group is private for residents only.

NextDoor

NextDoor is a private social network for you, your neighbors and your community. It's the easiest way for you and your neighbors to talk online and make all of your lives better in the real world. And it is free!

People are using NextDoor to:

  • Quickly get the word out about a break-in
  • Organize a Neighborhood Watch Group
  • Track down a trustworthy babysitter
  • Find out who does the best paint job in town
  • Ask for help looking for lost dog
  • Find a new home for an outgrown bike
  • Finally be able to call your neighbor by their first name
  • Alerts of upcoming meetings or events
  • Coordination and notification of events and activites

To join NextDoor, visit nextdoor.com or download the app! You'll need to enter your email address, street address and zip code. Then be sure to join the Park Ridge Community Association Group!
