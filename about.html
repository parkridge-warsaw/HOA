About Park Ridge

Park Ridge is a 134 property neighborhood located on the South side of Warsaw, Indiana. Construction in the neighborhood first began in 2005, with 105 properties developed by October 2016.

Park Ridge is governed by a Board of Directors representing property owners, and oversees the maintenance of common areas with the neighborhood including a playground and water retention pond.



Statistics:

  • Number of Homes: 134
  • Minimum Lot Size: 8,750
  • Minimum House Size (1-story): 1,200
  • Minimum House Size (2-story): 1,500
  • Largest Home: 3,708 sq ft
  • Garage Size: 2-3 car
  • Minimum Driveway Width: 16-feet
